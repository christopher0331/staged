Our Mission

The National LeioMyoSarcoma Foundation (NLMSF) is dedicated to supporting patients, caregivers, and survivors affected by LMS through education, advocacy, and research funding.

We strive to enhance the quality of life for those affected by LMS and accelerate research toward effective treatments and ultimately a cure.

Dr. Edmund Bartlett - Soft Tissue Sarcoma Treatment

Date: April 2, 2025

Time: 3:30 PM - 5:00 PM (ET)

Location: Online Zoom Webinar

Cost: Free

About This Event

Join Dr. Edmund Bartlett for an informative presentation on current soft tissue sarcoma treatment approaches. This webinar is designed for patients, caregivers, and healthcare professionals interested in the latest advancements in sarcoma care.

Speaker Background

Dr. Edmund Bartlett is a leading expert in soft tissue sarcoma treatment with over 15 years of specialized experience. He currently serves as the Director of Sarcoma Surgery at a major cancer center and has published extensively on innovative surgical approaches for sarcoma patients.

Webinar Topics

  • Current surgical approaches for soft tissue sarcomas
  • Multimodal treatment strategies
  • Post-surgical recovery and rehabilitation
  • Latest research in surgical oncology for sarcomas
  • Q&A session with audience

Registration Details

Attendance is free, but registration is required. All participants will receive access to the recorded webinar and additional resources following the event.

Dr. Marissa Nucci - LMS Research

Date: April 21, 2025 (Tentative)

Time: 3:30 PM - 5:00 PM (ET)

Location: Online Zoom Webinar

Cost: Free

About This Event

Dr. Marissa Nucci will present the latest developments in Leiomyosarcoma (LMS) research, with a focus on pathology, molecular characterization, and implications for treatment. This session is valuable for patients, caregivers, and medical professionals who want to understand the current state of LMS research.

Speaker Background

Dr. Marissa Nucci is a distinguished pathologist specializing in gynecologic and soft tissue pathology with a particular focus on leiomyosarcoma. Her groundbreaking research has contributed significantly to the understanding of LMS pathology and molecular signatures.

Presentation Topics

  • Current understanding of LMS pathology
  • Recent advances in molecular characterization
  • Research-driven approaches to LMS treatment
  • Ongoing clinical trials and research initiatives
  • Interactive Q&A with attendees

Registration Information

Registration link will be available soon. This event will be recorded and made available to registered participants after the presentation.

Caregiving Counts

Date: April 29, 2025

Time: 5:00 PM - 6:30 PM (ET)

Location: Online Zoom Meeting

Cost: Free

About This Event

"Caregiving Counts" is a special support session designed specifically for those caring for loved ones with LMS. This interactive workshop provides practical strategies, emotional support, and connection with others who understand the unique challenges of the caregiver journey.

Session Highlights

  • Self-care strategies for caregivers
  • Managing the emotional impact of caregiving
  • Navigating medical systems effectively
  • Building a support network
  • Open discussion and shared experiences
  • Resource sharing

Facilitated By

This session will be facilitated by experienced caregiver advocates and mental health professionals who specialize in supporting those who care for cancer patients. The format encourages participation and connection in a safe, supportive environment.
