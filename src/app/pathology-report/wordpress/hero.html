Why Your Pathology Report Matters
The pathologist's analysis of tumor tissue is an absolute critical starting point in a bottom line accurate diagnosis, in order to plan for the appropriate treatment protocol. Diagnosing soft tissue sarcomas can be challenging even with adequate biopsies or resection specimens.
